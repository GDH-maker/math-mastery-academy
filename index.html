How It Works

Built to Help You
Actually Learn

Not just a question bank — a complete adaptive learning system designed around how students actually improve.

📈
Adaptive Difficulty
10 levels from Novice to Royal Scholar. Problems automatically get harder as your accuracy improves, keeping you in the zone of growth.
📦
Box Method Hints
Factoring problems reveal a fully worked AC-method box diagram when you ask for hints — showing every step visually, not just the answer.
📊
Visual Math
Coordinate grids for distance and midpoint, slope diagrams, shaded region shapes, bar charts, and transformation graphs — answers hidden until you choose.
💡
Step-by-Step Hints
Three guided hint steps for every problem that walk you through the process without giving away the answer — learn how, not just what.
🧮
Built-in Calculator
A floating calculator (add, subtract, multiply, divide, √) is always available in the corner — just like the one allowed on the real TSIA2.
📧
Teacher Reports
Automatic weekly progress reports emailed to your teacher. Includes accuracy %, topic breakdown, level achieved, and problems to review.
🏆
Royal Title System
Earn scholar titles as you reach mastery on each topic — from Novice to Royal Scholar. The 80% mastery rule triggers a level-up celebration.
🎓
Khan Academy Links
Every topic links to curated Khan Academy videos so you can watch an explanation before you practice.
